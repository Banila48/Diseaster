  Rationale behind weightage
Gameplay takeaways (0.3) Based on our survey results, majority of the responses were cautious and inquisitive. Therefore, we have placed a high weightage on the ability of the game to convey important information that is sought after by the general public.
Gameplay complexity (0.3) Our product has to reach out to all the different population demographics. Therefore, it is vital that our game is simple enough for anyone to understand to impart the necessary knowledge to everybody. This ensures that no group will suffer during a crisis.
Fun factor (0.2) Although the fun factor is linked to the abiltity for our product to convey its message, we believe that in times of pandemics, people will play the game regardless of their enjoyment level as they gain crucial information on preventive measures
Adaptability (0.1) Adaptibiltity is of lower importance to us as our main goal is the preventive measures taken to minimize the spread/negative impact of pandemics
Cost (0.1) From our survey, we deduce that cost would be a more negligible factor since most people are merely concerned with their welfare. People would seek to purchase the product in times of crisis, regardless of the cost, as they value their safety more than expenses.
